# Novelty Decision

Chosen thesis: Tactile-Language Grounding Debt turns the seed bet into a mechanism: measure what language-conditioned policies fail to ground until touch resolves it.

New central mechanism: estimate task-relevant grounding debt after language and vision, choose tactile probes that repay the debt without excessive damage/cost, then update the action belief.

Decision: proceed as `STRONG_REVISE`. The v4 local benchmark supports a promising mechanism, but real tactile-language robot evidence is required before any ICLR-main submission.
